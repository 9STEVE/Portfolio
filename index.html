Hospitali Plus

H+ is a fork of Hospital Run project, who's aim is to make medical services available to developing countries. At Magnum, we contributed to the project by customizing it to fit in the Kenyan market and work seamlessly. We also integrated the HMIS with NHIF card payment system(Smart-link) so that patients can make payments easily. The HMIS is a PWA that works offline and online, ensuring that even in system failures or lack of network connection, the application works.
