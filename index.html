About

Let me introduce myself.

Am a citizen of the beautiful land of Kenya. I attended Taita Taveta University to purse a Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics and Computer Science, but after realizing that schools teach you just for the grades, I made it my responsibility to learn the new technologies and industry standards which meant I had to become a self-taught web developer. This is my second year as a web developer using Node.js and other new technologies to fulfil my passion, develop solutions and empower people around me. My strengths include adapting and working with edge-bleeding technologies in the field and developing custom solutions for the most rare scenario. My weakness is that I can't work without some EDM music playing :-) That's a brief summary about me, scroll down for more details.
